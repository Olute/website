    About me

    Hi my name is Tola Ayeni, mum to a gorgeous teenager. I enjoy reading and singing and I’m a great believe in life-long learning. I have spent a few decades in financial services in roles including audit, advisory and compliance. With a background in accounting and financial services, I have a strong analytical background and I have been fascinated by the world of computers and the trend in digital transformation has made it a necessity to retrain in order to be relevant. In this regard I have undertaken training in business analysis, understanding the impact of GDPR on IT projects. I have concluding a level 2 developer course in coding with a view to getting into an apprenticeship role as a junior developer.

    My interest in technology

    My journey into the programming world spans several decades, I had an interest in computers and technology and wanted a degree in computer science but due to circumstances beyond my control I ended up with a degree in Agricultural Economics. So I have spent a few decades in financial services as an auditor, accountant and financial adviser in both Nigeria & the United Kingdom. I have worked with top organisations including Ernst & Young, Citibank, NatWest Bank, HMRC, NHS, Edward Jones, Lloyds Banking Group & Accenture. In that time I have identified a huge gap between what providers of financial services are “selling” and what consumers require. I came into coding as I was passionate about setting up an application that would help clients with financial education, without the need to buy any product, developing a positive money mindset and providing a database of carefully selected providers that would support clients to achieve their goals.
